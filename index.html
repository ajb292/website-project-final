Built for distributed
friends & family

We live in an age where the people closest to us often live farthest away. This means that to see each other, and spend quality time together, we need to coordinate months in advance and somehow align our conflicting schedules. This is pretty hard to do when we don't have any visibility into each others' schedules. It requires a ton of back-and-forth to piece together the information.

Wouldn't it be easier if we just had instant access to each other's most up-to-date travel calendars? Wouldn't we eliminate the need for so much back-and-forth? Enter Whereabouts.

Wherabouts exists to get everyone on the same page and help bridge the distance that keeps loved ones apart. With Whereabouts, everyone's travel plans are finally shareable and searchable. We make it easier to follow each other's whereabouts and align your travel plans so you cross paths. We shouldn't have to go years without seeing our college roommate or our godmother.
